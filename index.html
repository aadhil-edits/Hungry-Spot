Our Story

Taste the soul of
Southern Sri Lanka

Hungry Spot was born from a simple belief — that Sri Lanka's greatest food shouldn't be hard to find. Nestled in the heart of Matara, we bring the bold, aromatic flavours of the southern coast to your plate, made fresh every single day.

From the crackling kottu to a generous banana leaf rice spread, every dish carries the spirit of our island. Good food, honest prices, a place where everyone belongs.
